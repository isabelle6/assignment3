Company

Serving the Community for 25 Years

We here at Guitar Shredders are committed to helping out the community. Since 1989, every year we donate guitars to music programs in different schools in the community. Twice a year we have Play to Help week, where we donate 50% of the proceeds from that week and donate it to school music programs.

John Doe founded Guitar Shredders in 1984, after being frustrated for so many years with other guitar distribution companies. He did not like the fact that if you wanted a nice quality guitar, you had to spend a lot of money. After lots of planning and hard work, Guitar Shredders was born.
